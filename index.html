About Us

Your Vision - Our Mission

Midlex Consulting Partners is a global consulting firm based in Pakistan and the USA, offering end-to-end services in legal advisory, company incorporations (LLCs, NGOs, Societies, Trusts), estate planning, taxation, corporate structuring, ADR, litigation, IT solutions, business investment, and global visa consultancy. We also provide healthcare advisory, CSR services, and tourism development — empowering individuals, businesses, and institutions to grow, comply, and lead with impact

A Legacy of Integrity & Impact Since 2016

Founded in 2016, and registered with SECP in Pakistan and as an LLC in the State of Texas, USA, Midlex Consulting Partners has built a reputation for professionalism, integrity, and results-driven execution. Our team of legal experts, consultants, and industry professionals bring deep knowledge, global exposure, and local insight to every engagement.

Transforming Ideas Into Impact

Whether you're looking to incorporate a business, resolve a legal dispute, register an NGO, launch a medical venture, explore tourism through our exclusive resort partnerships, or drive a CSR initiative—Midlex is your trusted partner every step of the way.

Our Services

Legal Services & Litigation

Midlex Consulting Partners provides a comprehensive range of legal services across various sectors. Our legal team delivers strategic and compliant solutions, supporting clients in legal structuring, contract drafting, regulatory compliance, and representation in mergers, acquisitions, and joint ventures.

Company, NGO, Society & Trust Registration

We guide clients through registration of companies, NGOs, societies, and trusts. Services include name reservation, document preparation, SECP compliance, and legal advisory throughout the formation and post-incorporation stages.

Business Formations & Global Expansion

We assist in forming and expanding businesses in Pakistan, USA, UAE, UK, and beyond. Our team handles registration, licensing, documentation, and strategic structuring tailored to local and global regulations.

Immigration Services

Midlex Consulting Partners offers professional immigration services for individuals, families, students, and professionals seeking to travel, study, work, or settle abroad. Our experienced immigration consultants and legal advisors provide end-to-end support tailored to the immigration laws and procedures of each destination country.

Solar Energy Solutions and Project Hunting Services

Midlex Consulting Partners is committed to promoting sustainable development through strategic support in renewable energy and project hunting. We facilitate solar energy solutions and help clients identify high-impact investment and development projects across various sectors.

IT Solutions & Digital Transformation

We offer software development, mobile and web apps, ERP/CRM systems, and digital strategy consulting to help businesses thrive in the digital landscape.

Medical Services & Online Consultations

Our healthcare services include hospital consultancy, online consultations, licensing, policy development, and risk management through certified medical professionals.

CSR & Social Justice Projects

We design and implement CSR programs focusing on education, health, environment, women empowerment, and child rights in alignment with development goals.

Tourism Services via Partner Resorts

Through our joint venture hospitality network, we offer customized travel, cultural excursions, wellness retreats, and luxury resort stays with regional development focus.
